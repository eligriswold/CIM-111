My name is Eli Griswold, and I am a junior Motion Pictures major at the University of Miami. I began my college education at Boston University in 2017 before transferring to Miami in the fall of 2018. Throughout college, I have studied a variety of different fields, including print and broadcast journalism, film production and writing, as well as sports administration.

However, while I am a film major, my interests range far beyond that. I have always been a well-rounded person, and have enjoyed creating different forms of content my whole life. Aside from writing and film, I used to make *bad* music with my friends in middle school, and recently revisited this hobby this past year, developing my music production skills. I have also always enjoyed taking photos, particularly of nature and run-down buildings.

This website is meant to display the various content I've created so far, and I either took or created every picture (other than my profile photo) on this site. Enjoy!

Photography

While my interests have varied throughout my life, I have always been drawn to photography. In the past, I have experiemented with portraits, but I am mainly interested in landscape and nature photography. Click the "Photography" section at the top of the page to view some of my work!
