Examples of My Work

In high school, I completed a number of different photography projects, but most of them got erased at the end of the semester after a computer malfunctioned in the studio. (This was before I learned the wonders of external hard drives, but you live and you learn). Below are some examples of my photography.

To the left is perhaps my favorite photo I've ever taken, titled "Division." I've used this photo as my desktop since I first took it in my junior year of high school.

What really draws me to this photo not only how I captured the wave crashing over a rock in the perfect manner, but that it almost divides the frame exactly in half. (Which inspired the name).

Beyond being an interesting photo to look at, the fact that it has been my desktop for so long makes it a good conversation starter. Some of my closest photography friends have come up to me saying they liked that photo, leading me to make some genuine connections.
